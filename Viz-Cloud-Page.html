Cloudiness


There is a scattered range of cloudiness seen globally at this time of year. Although there is a cluster of cloudiness seen in the northern hemispere, which may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was generated. There is no strong relationship between latitude and cloudiness, however, it is interesting to see that a strong band of cities sits at 0, 80, and 100% cloudiness.
